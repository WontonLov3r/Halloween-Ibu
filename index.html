Jigsaw

Movie franchise that shaped 21st century horror

Evolution of Jigsaw

Saw 1 starts with two men chained to pipes at opposite ends of a bathroom. They soon realize that they have been chosen to be a part of a game hosted by a serial killer by the name of Jigsaw. They have to win the game in order to stay alive.

In Saw 2, Detective Eric Matthews is given the case of finding Jigsaw, who is a well known killer at this point. They find out that he has trapped 8 more victims in a house to continue his games where loss is equal to death. Either Matthews has to find Jigsaw, or the victims have to win the game.

In Saw 3, Jigsaw kidnaps a doctor, Lynn. She is tasked with keeping him alive until his next victim can complete the new game in store for him. His next victim, Jeff, is a father who lost his son in a drunk driving accident.

Origin

Originally John Kramer, had originally led a normal life. John Kramer, who was formerly an engineer had a frontal tumor which was inoperable. As a result of unsuccessful attempted suicide, his near-death experience brought to him a whole new perspective of life. How they met their demise He supposedly died at the end of Saw 3, when he was shot by one of his victims. The rest of the movies were continued by other people who decided to take on his legacy and keep the saw games continuing. Famous Accomplishments Jigsaw had a toll of 86 victims in total, 55 or 57 of whom have failed their tests while the other 22 were successful
